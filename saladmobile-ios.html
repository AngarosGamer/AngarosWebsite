Salad Mobile iOS

Made by NullCube#0884 - Last update 10/10/2022

  • 1. Spend your Salad balance conveniently!
  • 2. View your Salad earning!
  • 1. Internet Connection
  • 2. Mobile device running iOS 7, iPadOS 13 or newer.
  • 3. Large screen device like iPhone XR or newer (excluding iPhone SE)
  • 4. Have a Salad account
  • Open Safari.
  • Go to app.salad.io
  • Tap the Bookmark icon at the bottom of the screen.
  • Tap Add to Home Screen.
  • Close Safari and open Salad from your home screen.
  • Rotate your device from vertical to horizontal.
